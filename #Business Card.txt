Prompt for business card 
You are an expert UI/UX designer and frontend developer specializing in professional portfolio design and digital business cards. Create a stunning, interactive digital business card for Rahaf Rababah, a Product-Oriented Software Engineer, that showcases her three signature projects:

1. **AI-Powered Image Enhancer** (Graduation Project 2025) - CNN model in PyTorch with 1000+ images
2. **WebTeb Symptom Checker Landing Page** - Frontend product-driven design with stakeholder collaboration
3. **Ramadan AI Chatbot MVP** - Full-stack AI chatbot with React, TypeScript, and Google Gemini integration

## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:

### Visual Identity:
- **Color Palette:** Use a sophisticated gradient from #2C3E50 (dark blue/primary) to #18A497 (teal/secondary) with #F39C12 (orange/accent) for highlights
- **Typography:** Use 'Inter' font family with elegant font weights (300-700)
- **Style:** Modern glassmorphism with subtle tech patterns, professional yet innovative

### Layout Structure:
1. **Header Section:** Name, title "Product-Oriented Software Engineer", and location (Amman, Jordan)
2. **Project Showcase Grid:** Three distinctive cards/badges highlighting each project with:
   - Project icon/emoji representing its purpose
   - Project name and brief 5-word value statement
   - Tech stack indicators (colors matching brand palette)
3. **Contact Hub:** Email and phone with copy functionality simulation
4. **Social & QR:** LinkedIn, GitHub links and a visual QR code element
5. **Skill Clusters:** Floating expertise tags that highlight on hover

### Interactive Elements:
- Each project card should have a subtle hover animation that reveals more details
- Skill tags should pulse gently and expand on hover
- Overall card should have a 3D lift effect on hover
- Add a "View Full Portfolio" interactive footer

### Project-Specific Details to Include:

**Project 1 - AI Image Enhancer (Grad Project):**
- Icon: 🎓 or 🖼️
- Tag: "PyTorch CNN | 1k+ Images"
- Highlight: "Computer Vision Specialist"

**Project 2 - WebTeb Symptom Checker:**
- Icon: 🏥 or 📱
- Tag: "React | Figma | Stakeholder-Led"
- Highlight: "Product-Driven Frontend"

**Project 3 - Ramadan AI Chatbot:**
- Icon: ☪️ or 🤖
- Tag: "React | Gemini | RAG Pipeline"
- Highlight: "Full-Stack AI MVP"

### Additional Requirements:
- Ensure responsive design for mobile viewing
- Include a subtle reference to "vibe coding" and AI tools expertise
- Add an interactive element that shows your hackathon achievement (MBMedxReact startup selection)
- Make it feel like a mini-portfolio, not just a contact card
